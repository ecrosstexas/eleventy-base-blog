## Reported Issue

- Short or erratic brew cycle(s).

## Troubleshooting

- Step 2 - Check the [water calibration](/kb/brewing/check-water-calibration/).

- Step 3 - Check the [sweetener calibration](/kb/brewing/check-sweetener-calibration/).
